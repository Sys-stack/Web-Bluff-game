BLUFF

RULES
1. Each player is dealt cards facedown.

2. On your turn, place one or more cards facedown and declare them (e.g., "Two Jacks").

3. Other players can either accept it or call "Bluff!"

4. If you're lying and get caught, you take the whole pile. If not, the accuser takes it.

5. The first player to get rid of all their cards wins.
